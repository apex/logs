# get_searches

The `get_searches` method returns all saved searches in a project.

  Inputs:

__Name__ | __Type__ | __Description__
--- | --- | --- | 
`project_id` | __string__ | The project id.

  Outputs:

__Name__ | __Type__ | __Description__
--- | --- | --- | 
`searches` | __array__ of [Search](../types/Search.md) | The saved searches.
