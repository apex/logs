# add_notification

The `add_notification` method creates a new notification.

  Inputs:

__Name__ | __Type__ | __Description__
--- | --- | --- | 
`notification` | [Notification](../types/Notification.md) | The notification.

  Outputs:

__Name__ | __Type__ | __Description__
--- | --- | --- | 
`id` | __string__ | The notification id.
