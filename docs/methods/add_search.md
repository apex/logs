# add_search

The `add_search` method creates a new saved search.

  Inputs:

__Name__ | __Type__ | __Description__
--- | --- | --- | 
`search` | [Search](../types/Search.md) | The saved search.

  Outputs:

__Name__ | __Type__ | __Description__
--- | --- | --- | 
`id` | __string__ | The saved search id.
