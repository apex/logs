# get_alerts

The `get_alerts` method returns all alerts in a project.

  Parameters:

__Name__ | __Type__ | __Description__
--- | --- | --- | 
`project_id` | __string__ | The project id.

  Results:

__Name__ | __Type__ | __Description__
--- | --- | --- | 
`alerts` | __array__ of [Event](../types/Event.md) | The alerts.
