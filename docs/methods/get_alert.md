# get_alert

The `get_alert` method returns an alert.

  Parameters:

__Name__ | __Type__ | __Description__
--- | --- | --- | 
`alert_id` | __string__ | The alert id.
`project_id` | __string__ | The project id.

  Results:

__Name__ | __Type__ | __Description__
--- | --- | --- | 
`alert` | [Alert](../types/Alert.md) | The alert.
