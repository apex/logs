# get_tokens

The `get_tokens` method returns all tokens in a project.

  Inputs:

__Name__ | __Type__ | __Description__
--- | --- | --- | 
`project_id` | __string__ | The project id.

  Outputs:

__Name__ | __Type__ | __Description__
--- | --- | --- | 
`tokens` | __array__ of [Token](../types/Token.md) | The tokens.
